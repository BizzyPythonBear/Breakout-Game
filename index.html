Instructions and Info

  • To move the paddle you use the left and right arrow keys.
  • Bricks with chains and locks on them take two hits to break and give 4 points.
  • Blue Bricks give 2 points
  • Regular brown bricks give 1 point
  • If the ball goes past the paddle, you lose.
  • To restart the game, refresh the page.
